Welcome to Bob's Sports

We offer one of the largest and most diverse selections of sports collectibles, memorabilia, and apparel in Pennsylvania. We are located at the Park City Center mall in Lancaster, Pennsylvania.

We have a wide range of officially licensed sports merchandise available. Some of our items include sports cards, autographed memorabilia, jerseys, shirts, hats, blankets, lamps, clocks and so much more. 100% of the merchandise that we carry is officially licensed by the NFL, MLB, NHL, NBA and NCAA. Check out our merchandise page for some of the products we have to offer.

Store Info

Bob's Sports is open Monday through Saturday from 10:00 am to 9:00 pm, and on Sundays from 11:00 am to 6:00 pm. If you are traveling to the area, please click here for directions. We are located off of the Harrisburg Pike exit on Route 30 at the Park City Center mall. Our store is located in the Boscov's wing of the mall, directly next door to Shogun Japanese Steakhouse.
