Store Info

Bob's Sports is open Monday through Saturday from 10:00 am to 9:00 pm, and on Sundays from 11:00 am to 6:00 pm. If you are traveling to the area, we are located off of the Harrisburg Pike exit on Route 30 at the Park City Center mall. Our store is located in the Boscov's wing of the mall, directly next door to Shogun Japanese Steakhouse.
